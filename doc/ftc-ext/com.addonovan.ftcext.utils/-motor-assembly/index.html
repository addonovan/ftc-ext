MotorAssembly

class MotorAssembly

A motor assembly is the programmatic representation of an assembly of any type of motor and its components which allows for higher level control of encoder movements.

Parameters

Motor - The motor used in this assembly.

WheelDiameter - The diameter of the wheel used in the assembly (in cm). Default is 4 inches/10.16 cm.

GearRatio - The gear ratio in this assembly (default is 1:1). This should be the number of spins of the motor divided by the spins of the wheel at the end.
