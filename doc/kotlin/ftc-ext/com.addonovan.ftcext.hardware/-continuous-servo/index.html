ContinuousServo

@HardwareExtension(Servo) open class ContinuousServo : Servo

A class which serves to distinguish a regular servo from a continuous (aka 360°) one. A few additions are made, such as the stop method and the StopPosition property, but its identical to a regular Servo for the most part.
